Privacy Policy

01

Information We Collect

Synlotech Technologies collects specific variations of data points to optimize our high-performance systems and development workflows. This includes:

  • Identity Elements: Name, business name, registration fields, and structural operational profiles.
  • Contact Points: Email addresses, telephone markers, physical office vectors, and active communication pipelines.
  • Technical Footprints: IP tracking, device telemetry configurations, connection duration logs via standard Google Analytics (G-4PMXTMQXM4).
02

How Data is Utilized

Your extracted payloads are explicitly used to fulfill development frameworks and system maintenance loops without causing processing latency. We use it to:

Deliver high-converting custom applications and custom portals.

Troubleshoot development packages, security bottlenecks, and server infrastructure bugs.

Monitor visitor behaviors using internal tracking layers to improve platform speed thresholds.

03

Third-Party Sharing

Synlotech Technologies enforces strict data isolation architectures. We do not sell, lease, or distribute your localized parameters to external marketing agents or third-party networks for advertising. Data transmission occurs strictly under the following isolated conditions:

  • Judicial Compliance: To comply with legal mandates under the United Republic of Tanzania Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA).
  • Core Infrastructure: To trusted database modules and cloud pipelines operating under non-disclosure execution terms.
04

Structural Security

We integrate multi-level defense measures across our digital architectures. Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) channels encrypt all raw text variables during interaction phases. Access keys to customer assets are dynamically locked out unless manual deployment procedures require explicit privilege elevation.

05

As a user or corporate client, you possess complete sovereign control over your underlying assets. Under regional data laws, you retain the right to query the extraction log, request absolute deletion from active server containers, restrict specific analytical parameters, or challenge flawed profiles.
